# Assistant Routing

Use the assistant that owns the primary decision or deliverable.

| Need | Primary Assistant | Common Review or Handoff |
|---|---|---|
| Company priorities, executive decisions, M&A, board issues | Executive | Legal, Compliance, Analytics |
| Product vision, features, roadmap, requirements | Product | Engineering, Design, Analytics |
| Code, architecture, security, infrastructure, Replit | Engineering | Product, Design, Compliance |
| Brand, UI/UX, graphics, presentations, visual systems | Design | Content, Marketing |
| Commercials, storyboards, video direction, AI video | Video | Design, Content, AI Studio |
| Copy, social posts, email, newsletters, PR, web copy | Content | Marketing, Compliance |
| Positioning, GTM, campaigns, audience, growth strategy | Marketing | Content, Analytics, Sales |
| Proposals, discovery, objections, demos, sales enablement | Sales | Product, Content, Legal |
| Shareholder messaging, public-company narrative, investor materials | Investor Relations | Compliance, Legal, Analytics |
| Regulatory risk, financial claims, public-facing review | Compliance | Legal, Content, Investor Relations |
| Contracts, terms, licensing, negotiation language | Legal | Compliance, Executive |
| SOPs, internal processes, implementation workflows | Operations | Engineering, Analytics |
| KPIs, analysis, dashboards, forecasting, measurement | Analytics | Executive, Marketing, Product |
| Search strategy, technical SEO, content optimization | SEO | Content, Engineering |
| AI prompts, generation workflows, agents, model instructions | AI Studio | Design, Video, Engineering |
| Music production, sonic identity, audio creative | Music | Video, Design |
| TAP Terminal workflows, product-specific operations and knowledge | Terminal | Engineering, Product, Content |

## Routing Rule

When a request spans several domains, the primary assistant owns the final deliverable and identifies required reviews. Do not split ownership merely because multiple disciplines are involved.
