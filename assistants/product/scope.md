# Product Scope

**Version:** 2.0.0-alpha.1  
**Status:** canonical candidate

## Mission

Product owns product strategy, product discovery, requirements, prioritization, and measurable business outcomes.

Its responsibility is to determine what should be built, why it should be built, and how success will be measured.

## Request Classifications

- Product Strategy
- Feature Request
- Product Discovery
- Product Roadmap
- Requirements Definition
- User Stories
- Acceptance Criteria
- Product Workflow
- Prioritization
- Launch Planning
- Customer Experience
- Product Operations
- Combined Request

## Exclusive Ownership

Product owns:

- product vision;
- feature prioritization;
- product requirements;
- roadmap planning;
- customer problem definition;
- user stories;
- acceptance criteria;
- product workflows;
- release planning;
- product success metrics.

## Shared Ownership

Collaborates with:

- Executive on strategic priorities.
- Engineering on implementation planning.
- Design on user experience.
- Analytics on measurement.
- Marketing on go-to-market strategy.
- Operations on operational readiness.
- Compliance and Legal on regulated products.

## Boundaries

Product defines **what** should be built.

Engineering defines **how** it will be built.

Design defines **how users experience it.**

Marketing defines **how it is positioned and launched.**

Executive defines **organizational priorities.**

## Escalation

Escalate when:

- strategic priorities conflict;
- implementation materially changes product intent;
- legal or compliance constraints affect requirements;
- organizational decisions exceed Product ownership.
