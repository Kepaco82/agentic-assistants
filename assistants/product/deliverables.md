# Product Deliverables

**Version:** 2.0.0-alpha.1  
**Status:** canonical candidate

## Purpose

Define the standard outputs Product should produce for common product requests.

Deliverables should be decision-ready, implementation-ready, measurable, and appropriate to the request.

## Product Strategy Brief

Include:

1. Strategic Objective
2. Customer Problem
3. Target Users
4. Market or Business Context
5. Product Opportunity
6. Recommended Direction
7. Key Trade-offs
8. Risks and Dependencies
9. Success Metrics
10. Next Decision

## Feature Definition

Include:

1. Feature Summary
2. Problem Being Solved
3. Target Users
4. User Value
5. Business Value
6. Functional Requirements
7. Non-Functional Requirements
8. User Flow
9. Acceptance Criteria
10. Dependencies
11. Risks
12. Success Metrics
13. Out of Scope

## Product Requirements Document

Include:

1. Overview
2. Background
3. Objectives
4. User Problem
5. Personas or User Segments
6. Scope
7. Functional Requirements
8. Non-Functional Requirements
9. User Experience Requirements
10. Data and Analytics Requirements
11. Operational Requirements
12. Compliance or Legal Considerations
13. Dependencies
14. Risks and Mitigations
15. Acceptance Criteria
16. Success Metrics
17. Rollout Considerations
18. Open Questions

## User Story

Use:

> As a [user type], I want [capability], so that [outcome].

Include:

- context;
- acceptance criteria;
- dependencies;
- edge cases;
- analytics requirements;
- out-of-scope items.

## Acceptance Criteria

Acceptance criteria should be:

- observable;
- testable;
- unambiguous;
- tied to the intended outcome;
- inclusive of important failure states and edge cases.

Use Given, When, Then formatting when it improves clarity.

7. Operational Readiness

## Product Decision Record

Include:

1. Decision
2. Date
3. Context
4. Options Considered
5. Selected Direction
6. Rationale
7. Trade-offs
8. Risks
9. Owner
10. Review Trigger

## Output Selection

Use only the deliverables required by the request.

Do not force every request into a full product requirements document when a smaller decision brief, user story, or recommendation is sufficient.
