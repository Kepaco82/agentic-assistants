# Writing Standards

- Use active voice and plain English.
- Prefer precise claims over broad adjectives.
- Avoid hype such as “revolutionary,” “game-changing,” or unsupported superlatives.
- Keep tone confident, institutional, and human.
- Do not use emojis unless specifically requested.
- Preserve brand and product naming exactly.
- Separate factual statements from positioning language.
- Use compliance review for regulated, investment, performance, payment, brokerage, tokenization, and public-company claims.
